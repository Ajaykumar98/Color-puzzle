                    WELCOME TO COLOR PUZZLE

                               WHAT IS IT?
Color puzzle is a simple and an interesting game developed to measure the reaction time.This is known as STROOP EFFECT named after John Ridley Stroop who first published about the effect in 1935.

                                   RULES
In this game you will be shown a colored text denoting a specific color with two buttons(OK and NO).And your job is click "OK" when you are shown with a text which is of the same color as itself and "NO" button when it is not.This game consists of three levels.
                                   LEVELS
Each level consists of FIVE questions.Levels 1,2 & 3 have a maximum limit of 5,4 & 3 seconds to answer.
